Are you baffled and frustrated by the process of trying to find and get signed by an agent or manager? Do you wonder why they won't look at your demo reel or read your scripts? Do you wonder how other people get agents and managers? Would you like to unlock the secrets to getting the attention of the industry insiders who can help move your career forward?

Then this seminar is for you!

Due to the overwhelming response to our last two seminars on this subject, and the requests to do it again…
On the evening of April 13 I’m offering a shorter version of our regular 4 hour seminar. 


The topics we'll cover are: 

   * What do agents look for in new clients? 
   * Timing: Are you ready for representation? 
   * How to prepare for representation 
   * Agent, manager, or both? 
   * Which agents are best for you? 
   * How to approach agents and managers 
   * Already represented?: Understanding the agent/client relationship 
   * How to navigate the business without representation 
   * Marketing yourself 
   * Managing your expectations 
   * How to handle unsolicited advice from friends, family, associates 
   * Producing your own product: Useful or not useful? 
   * Attitude: Why is it important? 
   * The secret to getting work in Hollywood 
   * And, of course, we'll have plenty of Q & A
